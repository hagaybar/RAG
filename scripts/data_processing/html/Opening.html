    • Opening Hours and Admittance

    updated: 08.09.2024

     

    Entrance to the library, as well as access to its open-shelf collections, certain databases (from computer terminals within the building), facilities, and selected services, are free of charge for any patron or visitor

     

    Sunday–Thursday 8:30AM–8PM
    Friday 8:30AM–12:30PM
    Saturday, Holiday Eves, Holidays CLOSED

     

    On the following Eves of holidays and public holidays (bank holidays) and national days the library is open and closes at 6:45PM:

    • Purim
    • Holocaust and Heroism Remembrance Day
    • Memorial Day for the Fallen Soldiers of the Wars of Israel and Victims of Actions of Terrorism
    • Tish'a B'Av (Ninth of Av)

     

    The library is also closed: for the duration of the Sukkot and Passover Chol Hamo'ed week and Extended summer vacation (last week of August)
